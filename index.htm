The Gateway

Granville, NC

Our luxury rental cabins are deluxe mountain homes with features you'd expect at a 4-star resort.

Our cabins feature plush living rooms, gourmet kitchens, large bathrooms and hot tubspool tables, air hockey and arcade games. All cabins feature one or more porches, and our cabin settings range from spectacular mountaintop locations with great views, to secluded locations with serene wood views.
